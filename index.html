Who Are We?

At Anmat Al Moharek, we’re driven by a passion for keeping heavy vehicles running smoothly, efficiently, and safely. Though we are a new player in the market, our foundation is built on years of industry knowledge and experience. Our founder, with over 8 years of hands-on experience in the heavy vehicle spare parts industry, has seen the challenges faced by fleet operators, repair shops, and individual vehicle owners. This deep understanding of the industry inspired the creation of a company focused on delivering quality, reliability, and affordability.
